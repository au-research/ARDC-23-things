The 23 (research data) Things poster lists each of the Things grouped by
like subjects.

-   [Download the
    poster](https://www.ands.org.au/__data/assets/pdf_file/0004/582484/23things_undated_poster_A4.pdf "23 (research data) Things undated poster")

<div id="new_content_container_571284">

### [![23 Things stickers](https://www.ands.org.au/__data/assets/image/0007/571282/23t-stickers.jpg "23 Things stickers"){width="99" height="140"}](https://www.ands.org.au/__data/assets/pdf_file/0004/571279/L6112C-Template.pdf "23 Things sticker template")Reward stickers

Reward yourself for each Thing you do with the specially printed 23
(research data) Things stickers. Every time you complete a Thing, take a
sticker and add it to your laptop or wear it proudly on yourself!

-   **Training/workshop groups**: email
    [contact@ands.org.au](mailto:contact@ands.org.au?subject=23%20Things%20stickers)
    with a postal address and how many sheets you need.
-   **Individuals**: can request a set of stickers by emailing
    [contact@ands.org.au](mailto:contact@ands.org.au?subject=23%20Things%20stickers).
-   **Print yourself**: [Download the sticker template (PDF, 0.62
    MB)](https://www.ands.org.au/__data/assets/pdf_file/0004/571279/L6112C-Template.pdf "23 Things sticker template PDF")
    and print onto **Avery L6112C** labels, which are available to order
    from major stationery suppliers.

### What's my pitch?

This document is a simple way to think about how to construct key
messages and possible responses that can be used to quickly and
succinctly pitch research data management to researchers. The responses
can be contextualised to suit your organisation. See [Thing 18: Data
interviews: talk the
talk](https://www.ands.org.au/working-with-data/skills/23-research-data-things/all23/thing-18 "Thing 18: Data interviews: talk the talk")
for how to use it in a workshop.

-   [What's my pitch? (PDF, 0.34
    MB)](https://www.ands.org.au/__data/assets/pdf_file/0005/568526/datalibrarians-toolkit.pdf "What's my pitch - PDF version")
-   [What's my pitch? (DOCX, 0.16
    MB)](https://www.ands.org.au/__data/assets/word_doc/0006/478041/datalibrarians-toolkit.docx "What's my pitch - Word version")
